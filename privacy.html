2. What data we collect

Fluello only collects data that is strictly necessary to operate the app:

  • Voice recordings: Processed exclusively on your device using iOS Speech Recognition (Apple). We do not store any recordings on our servers.
  • Learning progress: Your progress (level, streak, vocabulary) is stored locally on your device (SwiftData) and optionally synced via iCloud — subject to Apple's privacy policy.
  • Anonymous usage data (optional): Anonymized usage statistics may be collected via Apple's App Store Connect Analytics if you have enabled this on your device.
  • AI requests: Text inputs for AI-powered features (e.g. feedback, translations) are processed via Google's Gemini API. Google's privacy policy applies to this processing.

3. Purpose of processing

We process your data solely for the following purposes:

  • Providing and improving app features
  • Speech recognition and AI-based learning feedback
  • Storing your personal learning progress

4. Legal basis

Data processing is based on Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (performance of a contract) and Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interests).

5. Third-party services

The following third-party services are used:

  • Apple Inc. – iOS Speech Recognition, iCloud, App Store. Apple Privacy Policy
  • Google LLC – Gemini API for AI feedback. Google Privacy Policy

6. No sale of data

We do not sell, rent or share your personal data with third parties for advertising purposes. Data is only shared with the services listed above as part of the app's operation.

7. Data retention and deletion

Your learning progress is stored on your device. You can delete all data at any time by uninstalling the app or via the app's settings. Data is not retained longer than necessary.

8. Your rights

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access (Art. 15 GDPR)
  • Right to rectification (Art. 16 GDPR)
  • Right to erasure (Art. 17 GDPR)
  • Right to restriction of processing (Art. 18 GDPR)
  • Right to data portability (Art. 20 GDPR)
  • Right to object (Art. 21 GDPR)

To exercise your rights, contact us at: support@fluello.app

9. Right to lodge a complaint

You have the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection supervisory authority if you believe that the processing of your data violates the GDPR.

10. Changes to this policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy as needed. The current version is always available on this page.

11. Contact

For privacy-related questions: support@fluello.app
