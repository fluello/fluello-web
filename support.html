Support & FAQ

Frequently asked questions — answered quickly.

General

Who is Fluello best suited for?
Fluello is perfect for anyone who already "kind of understands" a language but freezes up when they actually have to speak. From complete beginners (A1) to advanced learners (C1), the app meets you exactly where you are and builds from there.
How is Fluello different from traditional language apps?
Most language apps rely on multiple-choice questions or vocabulary cards. Fluello has none of that. The real, spoken word is at the centre — followed by instant, intelligent feedback powered by Google Gemini AI. You speak, you get analysed, you improve. That's it.
What does Fluello cost — is there a free version?
You can download and use Fluello completely free. The free plan gives you one Daily Exercise (5 sentences) every day plus the first Speaking Trainer scenario ("Café"). For unlimited speaking practice, all scenarios and unrestricted AI feedback, you can upgrade to Fluello Pro at any time.
Which devices is Fluello available on?
Fluello is available for iPhone (iOS 17 or later). An iPad version is planned.

Technical

Speech recognition isn't working. What can I do?
Make sure you've granted the app microphone and speech recognition access. Go to iPhone Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone / Speech Recognition and enable Fluello. An internet connection is required for some features.
Can I use Fluello offline?
Some basic features (saved vocabulary, learning path view) are available offline. Speech recognition and AI feedback require an active internet connection.
How do I delete my data?
You can delete your data at any time via Settings → Profile → Reset in the app, or by uninstalling it. Your data is not stored on our servers.
