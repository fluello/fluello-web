Support & FAQ

Frequently asked questions — answered quickly.

General

Who is Fluello best suited for?
Fluello is perfect for anyone who already "kind of understands" a language but freezes up when they actually have to speak. From complete beginners (A1) to advanced learners (C1), the app meets you exactly where you are and builds from there.
How is Fluello different from traditional language apps?
Most language apps rely on multiple-choice questions or vocabulary cards. Fluello has none of that. The real, spoken word is at the centre — followed by instant, intelligent feedback powered by Google Gemini AI. You speak, you get analysed, you improve. That's it.
What does Fluello cost — is there a free version?
You can download and use Fluello completely free. The free plan gives you one Daily Exercise (5 sentences) every day plus the first Speaking Trainer scenario ("Café"). For unlimited speaking practice, all scenarios and unrestricted AI feedback, you can upgrade to Fluello Pro at any time.
What is Fluello?
Fluello is a language learning app for iPhone that trains you through real speaking practice and AI-powered feedback. You practice daily, get instant corrections and build vocabulary through realistic scenarios.
Which languages can I learn?
Fluello currently supports: German, Spanish, French, Italian, Portuguese and Japanese. More languages are planned for future updates.
Is Fluello free?
Yes, Fluello is free to download and use. All core features are available without payment. Optional premium features may be added in the future.
Which devices is Fluello available on?
Fluello is available for iPhone (iOS 17 or later). An iPad version is planned.

Learning & Features

How does the AI feedback work?
When you speak, Fluello analyses your answer for grammar, expression and accuracy. You instantly see what went well, what can be improved and a model answer for comparison. Feedback is powered by Google's Gemini API.
What is the learning path?
The learning path in Daily Exercise shows your exercise sequence as a visual snake path. You complete sessions, level up and track your progress from A1 to B2 on a single overview card.
What is the Vocab Box?
The Vocab Box stores words you've saved during training. Using spaced repetition, it surfaces due words for review at the right time so you actually remember them.
What is the Speaking Trainer?
In the Speaking Trainer you choose from real-life scenarios (café, hotel, doctor, job interview) and have a conversation with an AI partner. It's the best way to practice typical everyday situations in your target language.
What is the streak and how does it work?
Your streak counts how many consecutive days you've completed at least one practice session. The flame icon in the top right shows your current streak. Tap it to see your full activity calendar.
What is the lightbulb hint?
The lightbulb button next to the microphone gives you a suggestion for what you could say. It's especially useful when you're not sure how to answer a question or respond to a scenario.

Technical

Speech recognition isn't working. What can I do?
Make sure you've granted the app microphone and speech recognition access. Go to iPhone Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone / Speech Recognition and enable Fluello. An internet connection is required for some features.
Can I use Fluello offline?
Some basic features (saved vocabulary, learning path view) are available offline. Speech recognition and AI feedback require an active internet connection.
How do I delete my data?
You can delete your data at any time via Settings → Profile → Reset in the app, or by uninstalling it. Your data is not stored on our servers.
My streak was reset by mistake. What now?
Streaks are stored locally on your device. If you experience a technical issue, please reach out to our support team — we're happy to help.
How do I join the beta?
Sign up with your email on our home page. We'll send you a TestFlight invite. TestFlight is Apple's official beta testing platform — free to download, no special setup needed.
